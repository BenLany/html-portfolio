The Best Movies According to Bennett

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Interstellar

I love this movie because I am a huge space junkie. On top of that, the story telling and visuals are on a whole other level.

How to Train Your Dragon

How to Train your Dragon is the best animated film I have ever seen. The mix of emotion, world building and story telling is top tier.

Avatar

Avatar was released in 2009 and yet some how still has some of the best CGI that has ever been seen in cinema. On top of that, it contains a deep lore and about the world and lots of emotions.
